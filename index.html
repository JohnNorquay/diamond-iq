How to Play Diamond IQ

1
Pick your name from the roster, then tap PLAY BALL!
2
Choose a position to practice, or pick All Positions to play every spot.
3
Read the situation — check who's on base and how many outs there are. When you're ready, tap PITCH!
4
Watch the ball get hit to a fielder. Runners will start running!
5
Tap where you should go! Look for the gold X marks (cutoff and backup spots) and the blue glowing bases. Tap the right one before time runs out.
6
If the ball is hit to you, tap the player you should throw to. The other players will glow to show they're ready.
7
Watch the play finish! See if the throw beats the runner. You'll get points for being in the right spot.
The Game
⚾
Each game is 9 innings. You play a different position every inning.
3
Get 3 outs to end the inning. Infield plays can get outs — outfield hits are base hits.
🏆
Your total score from all 9 innings goes on the leaderboard. Be fast and accurate to score big!
Scoring
100 pts for correct positioning
Speed bonus — answer fast for up to 50 extra points
Streak bonus — get multiple right in a row for a multiplier
Tips
Cutoff = line up between the outfielder and the base
Back up = get behind the base where the throw is going
Cover = get on the bag and be ready for the ball
After every play, read the explanation — it tells you why you go there!
