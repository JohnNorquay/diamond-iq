# Diamond IQ - Defensive Positioning Rules

## How to Read This Document

Each scenario describes a game situation. For each scenario, every position has:
- **Action**: "GO TO" (move to a spot) or "FIELD & THROW" (you have the ball)
- **Target**: Where to go or who to throw to
- **Why**: The coaching explanation shown to kids

**Review each scenario and let me know what to change!**

| Code | Position | Default Location |
|------|----------|-----------------|
| P | Pitcher | Mound (center of diamond) |

## SCENARIO 1: Ground ball to Shortstop, NOBODY ON

| Position | Action | Target | Why |
|----------|--------|--------|-----|
| **SS** | **FIELD & THROW** | **Throw to 1B** | "Field it clean and throw to 1st for the out!" |
| P | GO TO | Back up 1st base | "The throw is going to 1st — back it up!" |
| C | GO TO | Stay at home | "Stay home and watch the field" |
| 1B | GO TO | Cover 1st base | "Get on the bag — the throw is coming to you!" |
| 2B | GO TO | Cover 2nd base | "Cover 2nd base in case of an overthrow" |
| 3B | GO TO | Cover 3rd base | "Stay on 3rd and watch the play" |
| LF | GO TO | Back up 3rd base | "Move toward 3rd in case a ball gets loose" |
| LCF | GO TO | Back up shortstop | "Come in behind where the play is happening" |
| RCF | GO TO | Back up 2nd base | "Move toward 2nd in case the throw goes there" |
| RF | GO TO | Back up 1st base | "Back up 1st in case the throw gets past!" |

## Notes for Dad

1. **These are simplified for 7U.** Real baseball has more nuance (number of outs changes everything, score matters, etc.). We can add outs-awareness later.
2. **The "cutoff" concept is the biggest thing to teach.** Most scenarios have at least one cutoff man.
3. **"Back up" assignments are the unsung hero.** Kids who aren't in the play still have a job!
4. **I assumed 4 outfielders**: LF, LCF, RCF, RF. Let me know if your alignment is different.
5. **Easy to add more scenarios later** — just tell me the situation and I'll write up all 10 positions.
